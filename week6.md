In conclusion
ARP resolves MAC addresses to facilitate communication within a LAN.
Default gateways facilitate network-to-network communication.
When it comes to routeing packets between subnets, routers are essential.
Both Layer 2 (ARP) and Layer 3 (Routeing) concepts were shown in the lab.
